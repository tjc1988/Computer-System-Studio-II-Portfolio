Alfred Hitchcock video edit project

This was for an assignment in the Hardware and Networking course in the Spring 2020 semester.

This assignment was related to the Lab 6 - Editing Video assignment

For this assignment, we had to use the video "Hitchcock Loves Bikinis" and use video editing
to make it look like he was looking at something other than what was in the original video
clip. We had to use at least three images and a music track.
