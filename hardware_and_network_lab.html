Hardware and Networking lab reports

This is a list of lab reports from the Hardware and Networking course in the Spring 2020 semester.



Lab 1 - Make a Website

For this assignment, we had to make a website with wordpress.com, which would be used for the later lab
lab assignments in this class.



Lab 2 - Structured Cabling

The objectives for this lab was to make a Cat5 patch cable, patch panel, and do research on other
kinds of networking cables.



Lab 3 - Basic Computer Hardware

The objectives of this lab was to disassemble a desktop computer, examine the components, reassemble the
computer, and then do research on other computing devices.



Lab 4 - Network Devices and the OSI Model

The objectives of this lab was to learn about the OSI model, look at several networking devices and find
out what exactly they were, do research on the Data Link Layer, and then use the command prompt to find
the details of the network.



Lab 5 - Exploring TCP/IP Networks

For this lab assignment, we had to make 2 separate networks, connect the 2 networks together with a router,
and then use ping tests to check the connection with the computers accross both networks.



Lab 6 - Editing Video

This was the lab assignment is related to the Alfred Hitchcock video edit project assignment.



Lab 7 - Apache Webserver

The objectives of this lab assignment download and use Apache Webserver, make a simple website, use Apache
Webserver to host the website for you local network, and use at least 1 other device on the network to view
the website.



Lab 8 - Packet Sniffing with Wireshark

For this assignment, we had to use Wireshark to record and read data and information on the traffic on our
networks at home though various activities.
