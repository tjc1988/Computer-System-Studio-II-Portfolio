Head swap project

This was for an assignment in the Computer System Studio 1 course in the Fall 2019 semester.

The goal of this assignment was to find a picture that had two or more people in it and use
Photoshop to switch at least two heads around onto other bodies. In this picture I switched
the two men's heads
