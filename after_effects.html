After Effects project

This was for an assignment in the Computer System Studio 1 course in the Fall 2019 semester.

For this project we had to use After Effects to create a short video clip. I used an image for
the background and then copied the ghost and werewolf from other images. I found the sound
for the ghost on a website and the background music was a track that I already had.
The effect of the lights flickering in the pumpkins and the bats blinking their eyes were done
be me also.
