Video montage

This was for an assignment in the Computer System Studio 1 course in the Fall 2019 semester.

For this assignment, we had to pick a movie or T.V. series and make a short "montage" video
using several clips from our selected movie or series. I chose the movie Tommy Boy. I found
the video clips on YouTube and used Power Director 15 for the video editing.
