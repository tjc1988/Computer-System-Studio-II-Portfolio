The Giver book project - Alternate ending

This was an assignment / project for my Acemdemic Conversations course in the Spring 2019 semester.

During the semester we had to read The Giver. For this assignment, we had to use The Giver as the topic and had
several choices of what kind of project we could use. I choose the option of creating an alternate ending and
had to read it to the class.
